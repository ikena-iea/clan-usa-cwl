How points and medals work

Each attack is scored using stars, destruction percent, and a Town Hall gap adjustment so hitting up in TH is worth more than farming a lower TH.

points = (stars + destruction% / 100) × TH-gap multiplier
TH-gap multiplier = 1 + 0.3 × (defender TH − attacker TH), clamped between 0.4 and 1.8

A missed attack is a flat −3 points, no matter the Town Hall matchup.

Example: a 3-star, 100% destruction attack against an equal Town Hall = (3 + 1.00) × 1.0 = 4.0 points. That's the benchmark for a textbook-perfect attack.

Medals are awarded based on average points per attack across the whole CWL, not the raw total, so it stays fair regardless of how many rounds have happened:

Gold avg ≥ 4.0 Silver avg 2.8–3.9 Bronze avg 1.5–2.7 — below 1.5, or missed attacks bring your average down
